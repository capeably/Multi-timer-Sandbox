Multi-Timer

A simple, ad-free multi-timer app. Create as many timers as you need, customize colors and sounds, and run them all simultaneously.

  • Add timers with the button or dropdown presets
  • Double-click a title to rename it
  • Edit time by clicking the hour/minute/second fields
  • Use the dropdown to pick time presets or sounds
  • Settings (⋮) to repeat, change color, or delete

All timers are saved in your browser automatically.
